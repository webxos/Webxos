LIFE@100 - Game Guide

Welcome to LIFE@100

Live a virtual life until age 100! Manage your money through work and random events to survive life's challenges.

How to Play

  • Click "Work" to earn $1 and start your life journey
  • Age increases every second until 100
  • Game ends if money reaches $0 or age hits 100
  • Click "Help" for this guide at any time

Random Events

Watch out for these events that affect your money:

  • Losses: Bills, repairs ($10-60)
  • Gains: Raises ($20-70), Lottery ($100-1100), Crypto ($50-550), Stocks ($30-230), Cash ($10-40)

End Game

When the game ends, you'll see:

  • Your final money total
  • A unique NFT number to commemorate your life (beta version: promotional use only)
