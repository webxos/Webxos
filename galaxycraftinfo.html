AI-Powered Cosmic Creation

At the heart of GalaxyCraft lies a TensorFlow-inspired procedural generation system. Our algorithms simulate the complexity of the cosmos, crafting:

  • Comets: Randomized semi-major axes (20-70 AU), eccentricities (0-0.8), and velocities (1-4 units/s) for authentic orbital paths.
  • Planetary Systems: Stars with 1-3 planets, each with unique orbital radii (10-40 AU) and velocities based on gravitational laws.
  • Galaxies: Spiral or elliptical clusters with 5-10 stars, radii from 50-150 units, and dynamic rotations for lifelike motion.

Unlike traditional simulations that rely on server-side processing, GalaxyCraft’s AI runs entirely client-side. By leveraging JavaScript and WebGL, we compute thousands of entity interactions in real-time, from orbital trajectories to collision physics. This approach ensures instant responsiveness and eliminates the need for costly backend infrastructure, making GalaxyCraft a pioneer in accessible, high-fidelity simulation.
