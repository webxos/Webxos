About Watchdog AI BETA

Watchdog AI BETA by WebXOS is a front-end tool monitoring web app energy use and carbon footprint. It drives sustainable software with eco-reports, collecting no personal data.

Use Case

Watchdog supports developers, companies, and green energy groups to cut digital carbon via:

  • Optimizing web apps for efficiency.
  • Auditing digital infrastructure.
  • Delivering eco-reports for IT.

How Tests Are Performed

Watchdog tests resource usage:

  • CPU Efficiency: 500K iteration stress test, scores time (4-8).
  • Memory Usage: Measures heap change (<2MB best) post-array allocation.
  • Power Usage: Estimates wattage (40W baseline), lower is better.
  • Battery Impact: Scores battery (>50% best), impacts carbon.

Tests yield Eco Score (0-100) and Carbon Footprint (kg CO2/h).

Sustainability Impact

Watchdog enables eco-friendly software by:

  • Real-time carbon monitoring.
  • Reducing device/data center energy.
  • Scaling for global net-zero goals.
  • Ensuring privacy, no data harvesting.
