About Watchdog AI BETA

Watchdog AI BETA by WebXOS is a front-end tool for monitoring web app energy efficiency and carbon footprint. It promotes sustainable software with real-time eco-reports, collecting no personal data.

Use Case

Watchdog aids developers, companies, and green energy groups in reducing digital carbon footprints via:

  • Optimizing web apps for energy efficiency.
  • Auditing digital infrastructure for sustainability.
  • Providing eco-reports for greener IT operations.

How Tests Are Performed

Watchdog runs tests to measure resource usage:

  • CPU Efficiency: Stress test (500K iterations) scores execution time (4-8).
  • Memory Usage: Measures heap size change (<2MB best) after array allocation.
  • Power Usage: Estimates wattage (baseline 40W), lower is better.
  • Battery Impact: Scores battery level (>50% best), affecting carbon footprint.

Tests yield an Eco Score (0-100) and Carbon Footprint (kg CO2/h).

Sustainability Impact

Watchdog drives eco-friendly software by:

  • Monitoring carbon in real-time.
  • Reducing energy in devices and data centers.
  • Scaling sustainability for global net-zero goals.
  • Ensuring privacy with no data harvesting.

WebXOS estimates a 20% digital footprint reduction.
