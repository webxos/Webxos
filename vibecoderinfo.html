WebXOS Platform

WebXOS is an open-source ecosystem for 2025, enabling minimalist, secure, and high-performance applications. VibeCoder, a flagship alpha project, showcases AI-driven creativity in a <50KB package. Explore the codebase at github.com/webxos/webxos, test the beta at webxos.netlify.app, and join the community on X at @injector_ai.

Features

  • Vibe Code: Craft precise prompts with a 5-question guide, tailoring project goals, complexity, and vibe.
  • Vibe Check: Analyze your coding mindset with a calculus-based AI model, offering personalized prompting tips.
  • AI Troubleshoot: Optimize prompts with clear feedback on brevity, verbosity, or missing action verbs.

Technical Details

VibeCoder is engineered for efficiency:

  • Language: Python (Skulpt-compatible)
  • Dependencies: None
  • Environment: Browser-based, injectable into injector.html
  • Security: Regex input sanitization
  • Logging: Outputs to vibecoder_log.md
