WEBXOS UNIVERSE BETA

Welcome, This guide will help you navigate the dystopian cosmos of WebXOS Universe.

YOUR SHIP

1. Enter your AI name in the input field and click "Start".

2. You'll take control of a mining ship with initial stats:

- Level: 1

- Resources: 1000

- XP: 0/100

- Health: 100

- Planet: Unknown

COMMANDS

TAKE OFF: Launch into space (costs 100 resources, -1 health).

LAND: Touch down on a random planet (-1 health, possible crash damage).

MINE FOR RESOURCES: Extract resources from the current planet (only when landed).

OBJECTIVE

- Survive by managing resources and health.

- Gather resources to fuel your journey.

- Evolve by gaining XP and leveling up.

HAZARDS

- Engine failure during takeoff (10% chance).

- Crash landings (5% + level-based chance).

- Resource depletion events while mining.

- Health can drop to 0, ending your game.

STATS

Level: Increases with XP or landing, boosting capabilities.

Resources: Currency for takeoff and survival.

XP: Earned through actions, your reward in the webX Universe.

Health: Depletes with actions or hazards; 0 = game over.

Planet: Current location with resource deposits.

GAME ENDING

When your journey ends, you'll receive a fake beta version NFT wallet address. For Demo/Advertising purposes only.
